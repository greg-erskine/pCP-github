How-to download piCorePlayer

1.

Go to the piCorePlayer Google Download web page—go to piCorePlayer Download ↗.

2.

Click on the "get it from here" link.

3.

Wait 5 seconds for the download to begin.

4.

Click on the [Save] or [Save as] button when prompted.

5.

Wait for the download to complete—about 20 or 30 seconds.

6.

Go to your local download location.

7.

Unzip the piCorePlayer image.
